Welcome to Purr Clicker!

In this game, your goal is to collect purrs by interacting with the cat and purchasing upgrades to increase your progress over time.

How to Play

  • Click on the cat to earn purrs.
  • Each click gives you purrs based on your current purrs per click value.
  • Use your purrs to buy upgrades from the Upgrade Shop.

Upgrades

  • Some upgrades increase the number of purrs you earn per click.
  • Other upgrades unlock and speed up automatic purring, which earns purrs for you over time.
  • Each upgrade has a cost, and the cost increases after it is purchased.
  • Only one auto-purring timer runs at a time — the fastest one active wins.

Rewards

  • 30 badges to collect! Hover over a badge to see what it takes to unlock it.
  • Locked badges appear greyed out. Earn them by reaching purr milestones, buying upgrades, and unlocking auto-purring.
  • When a reward is earned, the badge lights up and a short congratulations message is shown.

Scoreboard

  • The scoreboard shows your total purrs, purrs per click, and the number of upgrades you own.
  • These values update automatically as you play.
  • Have fun collecting purrs and unlocking all 30 rewards!
